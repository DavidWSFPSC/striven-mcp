The Problem

The gap between approval and execution

An approved estimate in Striven contains everything — labor charges, materials, disposal fees, taxes, and miscellaneous lines. The installer needs only a subset: the physical items that must be on-site for the job to happen.

Right now, translating an estimate into a field package requires manual review. Manifest Center automates that filter and produces a structured checklist the crew can actually use.

First Version Goal

Estimate → Field Manifest

Enter a Striven estimate ID. Pull all line items. Filter out labor, disposal, tax, and non-physical charges. Present the remaining physical materials as a structured field manifest — what needs to be ordered, staged, and confirmed before the crew goes out.

  1. Enter estimate ID

    Paste any approved Striven estimate number to pull its full line-item detail.

  2. Pull physical line items

    Retrieve all line items from Striven and identify materials — appliance, venting, fuel components, surround, hearth, fasteners.

  3. Filter non-material charges

    Strip labor, trip charges, permits, disposal, tax lines, and misc. fees. Only items that physically arrive on a truck or shelf remain.

  4. Generate field manifest

    Output a structured checklist: item name, quantity, model/SKU if available, and a status column for staging confirmation.

  5. PM review and handoff

    PM confirms all items are staged or ordered. Manifest is signed off before the job hits the schedule. Crew goes out ready.
