# WilliamSmith Fireplaces — Company Overview

## Who We Are

WilliamSmith Fireplaces (wsfpsc.com) is a fireplace sales, installation,
and service company based in Charleston, SC. The company operates across
the greater Charleston metro area including Mount Pleasant, Daniel Island,
James Island, Johns Island, West Ashley, Goose Creek, and Summerville.

**Company size:** 20–50 employees
**Owner:** William Smith
**Business type:** Specialty trade contractor — hearth and fireplace

---

## What We Do

### Sales
- In-home and showroom consultation
- Custom fireplace specification and quoting
- Builder/contractor relationship management

### Installation
- New fireplace installation (gas, wood-burning, electric)
- Gas log and burner system installation and replacement
- Chimney liner and chase installation

### Service & Repair
- Annual fireplace tune-up and inspection
- Gas system repair (valves, ignition, controls)
- Chimney repair, cleaning, and crown work
- Warranty and callback service

### Retail / Supply
- Fireplace doors, mantels, and surrounds
- Gas log sets and accessories
- Hearth pads and decorative elements

## Business Management: Striven

All sales orders, customer records, invoices, tasks, and scheduling are managed
in **Striven** (striven.com). WilliamSmith uses Striven as the system of record.

Key Striven concepts for WilliamSmith:
- Every job = a Sales Order (also called an Estimate internally)
- Customers are both homeowners and builders
- Custom fields classify job type (Product Type)
- Tasks are used for scheduling (preview visits, install appointments)
- Invoices are generated from completed estimates

---

## Revenue Drivers

1. **Builder / new construction volume** — high unit count, steady revenue
2. **Isokern and gas fireplace installs** — highest value per job
3. **Gas log replacement** — high frequency, moderate value
4. **Service contracts / annual service** — recurring, underutilized currently

## Known Revenue Leakage

- Missing Gas Log Removal Fee ($200/job) — most common estimating error
- Missing portal flag → customer can't approve → delays payment
- Approved jobs not scheduled promptly → customer frustration, potential cancellation
